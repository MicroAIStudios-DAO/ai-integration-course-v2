Pioneer Assessment

The Agentic Readiness Scorecard

Score whether your business is ready for AI automation across five critical dimensions. Takes about 5 minutes.

Assessment Dimensions

1. Data Maturity

Is your operational data structured, accessible, and API-ready?

2. Process Documentation

Are your repeatable workflows documented enough for an agent to follow?

3. Team Readiness

Does your team have the technical comfort level to adopt agentic tools?

4. Integration Surface Area

How many of your tools expose APIs that agents can connect to?

5. Governance Posture

Do you have guardrails for when AI makes decisions on your behalf?
